Baby Breeze App

  • Problem: Parents of children under two face challenges finding sensory-friendly and developmentally appropriate media for their babies. Existing apps often overstimulate children with bright colors, loud sounds, and fast movements. This leads to difficulties in calming routines, such as nap time, and reduces opportunities for cognitive and sensory growth in a relaxed manner.
  • Tools Used:
    • Sketching Tools: Pen and paper for low-fidelity prototypes.
    • Mind Mapping Software: Tools like Coggle to brainstorm ideas.
    • Prototyping Tools: Adobe XD for creating high-fidelity interactive prototypes.
    • Data Analysis Tools: Python for statistical analysis of usability testing results.
    • Survey Tools: Google Forms or manual collection for user surveys.

This project focuses on developing Baby Breeze, an application designed to provide parents with a calming, sensory-friendly media experience for their babies. The app enables parents to easily browse soothing content, set screen time limits, customize sensory settings, and access a help section for guidance. It also allows users to track session reports and give feedback, ensuring an interactive and personalized experience tailored to their baby’s developmental needs.
